# Social Content Workflow Instructions

## Input Context

Read these BMAD configuration files for context:

## Task Overview

Create engaging social media content using platform strategies, calendars, and community building techniques.

## Content Coverage

### Platform Strategies
- **LinkedIn** — Thought leadership, professional insights
- **Twitter/X** — Real-time updates, industry news
- **Instagram** — Visual storytelling, behind-the-scenes
- **Facebook** — Community building, events
- **TikTok** — Short-form video, trends

### Content Calendar
- **Thematic Planning** — Monthly/quarterly themes aligned with product
- **Posting Schedules** — Optimal times by platform and audience
- **Content Mix** — Educational, promotional, engaging, curated
- **Seasonal Campaigns** — Holidays, product launches, events

### Engagement Techniques
- **Community Building** — Responding to comments, DMs
- **User-Generated Content** — Reposts, testimonials
- **Interactive Content** — Polls, Q&As, live streams
- **Hashtag Strategy** — Branded, trending, industry

### Community Guidelines
- **Tone of Voice** — Consistent with brand personality
- **Content Standards** — What to post/not post
- **Crisis Management** — Negative comments, complaints
- **Moderation Policies** — Flagging, banning

## Workflow Execution

1. Read BMAD config and product brief for context
2. Select platform strategies and content mix
3. Develop content calendar with themes
4. Create engagement and community guidelines
5. Define performance metrics and KPIs

## Final Output

Write results to `{project-root}/_bmad-output/social-content-{date}.md` containing:

### Social Content Section
- **Social content calendar** with themes and topics
- **Platform-specific content formats** and guidelines
- **Engagement strategies and community guidelines**
- **Performance metrics and KPI tracking** with tools
