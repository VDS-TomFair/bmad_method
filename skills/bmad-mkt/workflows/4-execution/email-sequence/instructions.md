## Task Overview

Create engaging email sequences using lifecycle frameworks, personalization, and metrics tracking for lead nurturing.

## Content Coverage

### Email Sequence Frameworks
- **Welcome Series** — First touch, value introduction, onboarding
- **Nurture Campaigns** — Educational, problem-solving, proof-driven
- **Re-engagement Flows** — Win-back, feedback, special offers
- **Lifecycle Stages** — Lead, trial, customer, advocate

### Subject Line Techniques
- **Personalization Tokens** — Name, company, location
- **Curiosity Gaps** — Questions, teasers, exclusivity
- **Urgency and Scarcity** — Limited time, expiring offers
- **Benefit-Driven** — Value propositions, outcomes

### Audience Segmentation
- **ICP-Based Lists** — Job title, industry, company size
- **Behavioral Triggers** — Downloads, webinar attendance
- **Engagement Levels** — Active, passive, dormant
- **Lifecycle Status** — Prospects, trials, customers

### Copywriting and Personalization
- **Tone Mapping** — Formal/informal, serious/playful
- **Value Proposition Alignment** — JTBD, benefits
- **Social Proof Integration** — Testimonials, case studies
- **CTA Optimization** — Placement, clarity, urgency

## Workflow Execution

1. Read BMAD config and product brief for context
2. Select email sequence framework and lifecycle stage
3. Develop subject lines with personalization tokens
4. Write copy with tone mapping and value alignment
5. Define segmentation and behavioral triggers

## Final Output

Write results to `{project-root}/_bmad-output/email-sequence-{date}.md` containing:

### Email Sequence Section
- **Email sequence outline** with topics and timing
- **Subject lines and preview text** for each email
- **Personalization variables and segmentation** logic
- **Metrics and KPI tracking recommendations** with tools
