# Step 3: Positioning Statement

## Task

1. `{project-root}/_bmad-output/marketing-positioning-{date}.md` — Read Context and ICP sections

## Framework Application

Apply frameworks to define positioning:

### Jobs-to-be-Done (JTBD)
- What job does our product uniquely solve?
- What existing solutions do users fire to hire ours?
- What is the progress users seek?

### Blue Ocean Strategy
- What factors can we eliminate/reduce to open new demand?
- Where is uncontested market space?
- How can we make competition irrelevant?

Append to `{project-root}/_bmad-output/marketing-positioning-{date}.md`:

- **One-sentence value proposition** — Clear, differentiated, evidence-based
- **JTBD Evidence** — User job our product solves
- **Blue Ocean Insight** — Uncontested demand we address
- **Target ICP** — Who benefits most from this positioning
